Field Meridians Nature School Presents:
A TREE GROWS
IN BROOKLYN
A guided walking workshop exploring street trees

↓
Celebrate Arbor Day with a tree identification walk along Nostrand Avenue!

Get to know your tree neighbors, with your human (and more than human) neighbors. We’ll be checking in on our neighborhood street trees with a stroll up Nostrand Avenue to identify the trees that provide shade, ecologies, and homes for species of all kinds. We’ll be learning how to identify and care for trees, sharing stories of their cultural histories, and discussing our shared future with them in a time of unprecedented global ecosystems change.
When: Sunday, April 28 2024
Where: Eastern Pkwy & Nostrand Ave
Time: 10:00 am - 12:00 pm
Facilitator: Michael Cafiero
Cost: $15 suggested donation
Max # of Participants: 15
Field Meridians is an extension of MOLD magazine, the critically acclaimed online and print magazine about design and the future of food.
